Style Spotlight

88 Avenue is a digital publication dedicated to exploring the growing influence of Asian streetwear on Western fashion. Highlighting iconic staples such as Japanese denim and Onitsuka Tigers, we examine how traditional craftsmanship and contemporary design from across Asia are redefining global style and becoming essential elements of the modern wardrobe.
